Quick & Detailed Ordering

Users can place a quick order by simply selecting an address and time, or create a detailed order by adding clothing items by category. Prices are calculated automatically based on selected services such as washing, ironing, or folding.

Smart Scheduling System

The scheduling module prevents invalid selections by disabling past or fully booked time slots. This ensures smooth coordination between users, laundry facilities, and couriers.

Project Overview

An overview of the motivation, goals, and academic contribution of the WashHub mobile application.

Project Objective

WashHub provides a modern, mobile-first laundry service for everyone with busy or temporary lifestyles. Users can place laundry orders, select pickup and delivery times, and track the entire service in real time through a simple and intuitive interface.

The application is perfect for tourists, single residents, people whose washing machines are temporarily out of order, and busy urban professionals. With smart scheduling and automated price calculation, WashHub reduces waiting times and makes laundry care fast, convenient, and stress-free.
